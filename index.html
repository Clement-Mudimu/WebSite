About Us

Darlys Engineering

Darlys Engineering was created from the vision of a successful entrepreneur Darlington Masanganise with more than 10 years of experience in the welding and repair industry. After successfully helping to build a previous company from the ground up, Mr. Masanganise saw an opportunity for additional markets in 2018 and took this vision to the next level.

As a result, Darlys Engineering was opened for business in April 2018. It is more than a welding shop. It is a fully-integrated metal-working and repair facility, providing welding, fabrication, general repair services and on-site welding services. Additionally, it has the capacity to provide custom contract services. It has an extensive supply of steel materials on hand, which are available for retail to the general public.

How is Darlys Engineering diffrent?
We believe customer satisfaction is more important than large profits. With customer satisfaction comes repeat business - this in turn leads to more work, which assists the company's growth.

We are not only in the metal/steel business; we are also in the people business. In fact, one of our main goals is to forge relationships with our customers that are as strong as the steel we provide.

We do not only work for a variety of different industries; we also work for the general public. Our goal is to have your long-term business last long. We are committed to providing you with the highest level of customer service and quality, so you will always be happy that you chose us. We are blessed to have a broad base of loyal customers to serve. We look forward to add you to our customer base.
